ABOUT ME

My Journey in Journalism

I'm a passionate journalism student at Manipal University with a focus on human interest stories. My journey began when I discovered the power of storytelling during my high school years.

Throughout my time at Manipal University, I've had the opportunity to develop my skills in various areas of journalism, from investigative reporting to feature writing.

I believe that good journalism is about more than just reporting facts—it's about capturing the essence of human experiences and sharing stories that matter. Whether through written articles, photography, or multimedia presentations, I strive to create content that resonates with audiences and provides meaningful perspectives on contemporary issues.

When I'm not writing or editing, you can find me exploring new neighborhoods with my camera, attending local events, or engaging with community initiatives that align with my values of authenticity and social awareness.
